Fuzz

Helping independent musicians to find new partners and make more profitable shows

UX web dev

The main goal of this project was to help indepenent artists, to be more precise, indie musicians to make more profitable shows helping them to create connections between other artists due to the fact that the audience tend to pay more for small concerts when there are more than a single artist performing. After a huge research and after months of testing we developed an iOS mobile app. It was developed during the Apple Developer Academy @ 2017.
